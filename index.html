About Shalak Uganda Ltd

Empowering Smallholder Farmers in Northern Uganda

At Shalak Uganda Ltd, we are dedicated to transforming the lives of smallholder farmers by providing affordable, high-quality organic fertilizers, climate-smart seeds, and eco-friendly pesticides. We bridge critical gaps, helping farmers increase yields, improve food security, and foster economic growth in their communities.

Your Questions, Our Answers

Everything You Need to Know

What kinds of products does Shalak Uganda Ltd provide?

We supply high-quality, sustainable agricultural inputs including **organic fertilizers**, **climate-smart seeds**, and **eco-friendly pesticides** to help you achieve a more abundant and resilient harvest.

How do your products help me with climate change?

Our **climate-smart seeds** are specifically chosen to be drought-resistant and resilient to changing weather patterns. We also advocate for sustainable land management and water-efficient methods to ensure long-term productivity.

Do you offer training and support for farmers?

Yes, **training and support** are central to our mission. We provide farmers with essential skills and technical guidance on sustainable practices and modern farming techniques to enhance their knowledge and success.

How can I get started with Shalak Uganda Ltd?

You can get started by reaching out to us directly through our **contact information** or by visiting our offices. We are dedicated to working closely with farmers across Northern Uganda to meet their unique needs.
