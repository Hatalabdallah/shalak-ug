About us

Our Mission to Empower Farmers

Shalak Uganda Ltd was founded to address the challenges facing smallholder farmers, including limited access to quality inputs and the effects of climate change. We are dedicated to providing the tools and knowledge needed to cultivate a resilient future.

Comprehensive Agricultural Solutions

We deliver affordable, high-quality organic fertilizers, climate-smart seeds, and eco-friendly pesticides, along with the training to use them effectively.
